Our Services

N-TRACS provides expert neuropsychological assessments and advice for medico-legal purposes. Available by appointment nationwide, N-TRACS has a strong team of highly qualified professionals working diligently to implement a quality service; providing clear and accurate assessments enabling legal procedures to be administered swiftly.



N-TRACS was first established in 2010 by the founder and Clinical Director of the Transitional Rehabilitation Units[www.trurehab.com], est. 1992, Dr Howard Frank Jackson.



Experienced in clinical neuropsychology for over 30 years , he has published and presented widely on the issues regarding this area of expertise.
